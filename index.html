ABOUT ME

I love 3D Modeling - Me in 2018

A self-taught 3D modeler, I had always been interested in the artistic and aesthetic aspects of video games. Coming from the Mechanical Engineering course in Singapore's Institute of Technical Education, I have developed a strong foundation in CAD and 3D modelling, blending technical skills with my passion for design.

Vyn Peregrine, The Necromancer
